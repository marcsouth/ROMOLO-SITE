LEARN TO SEE



“My vision is not only to teach you how to sculpt, but moreover, to teach you how to see, from that, everything else flows”



Romolo Del Deo



Studio Romolo is lead by artist and master sculptor, Romolo Del Deo. His artwork has won acclaim globally, honored by the Art Newspaper as one of the most important projects of the Venice Art Biennial 22. His teaching methods have received the Harvard-Danforth Award for Excellence in Education and the President's Award for Contributions to the Arts. The artwork of Romolo Del Deo is exhibited in public monuments, museums and galleries internationally and has received major awards and prizes. He is the founder of the Long Art Movement which is the cornerstone of his teaching method.

The Long Art



“I am dedicated to the idea of rethinking, reimagining and up-cycling the sustainable, resilient imagery and methodology from the ancient past to speak to the present.
The era of 20th Century fast, momentary, disposable ‘now’ art & culture is ending. Learn to create enduring, sustainable, ‘long’ art for the new millennium.”



Romolo Del Deo



Atelier



Studio Romolo Atelier is a privately operated independent art school, The Atelier is open to all, from beginners to advanced professionals alike, and offers training in sculptural creativity, idea building and artistic mastery within a studio environment. The Atelier workshops take place in two historically important and active art colonies, that have inspired artists for generations - Provincetown USA and Pietrasanta Italy.
